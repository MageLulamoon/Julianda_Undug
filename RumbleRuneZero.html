A mage's last stand against the dark.

HOW TO PLAY

  • Swap adjacent runes to match 3 or more.
  • Matches deal damage based on rune type vs monster type.
  • Chaining cascades may drop Tomes.
  • Monster attacks on a timer — survive and clear the dungeon.
  • Comboing the monster triggers RAGE MODE.
  • Tomes are one-use spells. You can hold up to 4.
  • OVERGROW converts Dark→Grass & heals 1/5 HP.
  • TIDAL RUIN treats Dark as Water & removes Rage.
  • Fire beats Grass  |  Grass beats Water  |  Water beats Fire
  • Dark is only super-effective vs Dark.
